Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park

Located in South Andaman, this park was established to protect mangroves, corals, and nesting sea turtles.

Campbell Bay & Galathea National Parks

Part of the Great Nicobar Biosphere Reserve, these parks feature tropical evergreen and semi-evergreen forests, and diverse wildlife including the giant robber crab and Nicobar pigeon.

Saddle Peak National Park

Home to Saddle peak, the highest point in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, this park boasts a variety of fauna including the Andaman wild pig and saltwater crocodile.

Rani Jhansi Marine National Park

Situated in Ritchie’s Archipelago, this park is known for its mangroves, evergreen forests, and a variety of marine life including dugongs, dolphins, and sea turtles.

Narcondam Island Wildlife Sanctuary

Narcondam Island is a dormant volcano.
